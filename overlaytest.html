  Welcome to Tradera

Tradera provides a safe crypto-currency escrow service for user transactions. A crypto-currency escrow is an arrangement between two parties where Tradera holds payment of the funds (Tradera) required to complete a transaction. The funds are kept secure until all of the terms of a transaction are met.

Arbitrator

Tradera protects buyers from fraudulent sellers by requiring the coin to be deposited up front, before any money changes hands. In the event of a dispute, Tradera service will act as the arbitrator and determine who should ultimately receive the coins.
